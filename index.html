About Us

"

Turtle Hill Farm is a family owned and operated business in the city of Woodruff, South Carolina. This testimony to the symbiosis between humanity and nature was founded in YEAR by Edward Williams, Patricia Williams, and their youngest son Ian. We emphasize an organic approach to cultivating land. All of our crops are grown pestiside free and non-GMO. Stop by and we will show you have to rejoin nature in a way that is non-invasive to the Earth.
